	We start with a very simple setup.Suppose we have a 2 dimensional Bravis lattice $\mathbb{Z}\boldsymbol{a_1}+\mathbb{Z}\boldsymbol{a_2}$.Copy the lattice to another layer and rotate a certain angle,one obtains another lattice
	$$R(\theta)(\mathbb{Z}\boldsymbol{a_1}+\mathbb{Z}\boldsymbol{a_2})$$
	We focus on the case when $\theta$ is small.When we stack the rotated layer and the original lattice,a new larger periodicity magically emerges.
